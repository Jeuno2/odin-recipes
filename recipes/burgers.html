Burgers Recipe

Finished Burgers Straight Off the Pit, Serves 8

Ingredients

  • hambuger buns
  • hambuger patties
  • lettuce
  • tomatoes
  • pickles
  • salt & pepper
  • mayo and/or mustards

Steps

  1. lay patties out on sheet
  2. season with salt and pepper
  3. place back in fridge until pit is ready
  4. light coals and bring up to temp
  5. add patties to grill until desired doneness is reached
  6. add mayo and/or mustard to buns
  7. add patty and toppings to buns
