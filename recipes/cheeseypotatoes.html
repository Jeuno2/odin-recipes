Cheesey Potatoes Recipe

Finished Cheesey Potatoes Dish, Serves 12

Ingredients

  • yukon gold potatoes
  • cheddar cheese
  • sour cream
  • cream cheese
  • salt & pepper

Steps

  1. peel potatoes
  2. boil potatoes
  3. mash or rice boiled potatoes
  4. mix cold ingredients
  5. add spices
  6. incorporate cold ingredients into potatoes but do not over-stir
  7. add to casserole dish and bake
