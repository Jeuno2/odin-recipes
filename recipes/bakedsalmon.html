Baked Salmon Recipe

Baked Salmon, Serves 3 to 4

Ingredients

  • salmon filets
  • butter
  • honey
  • dijon mustard
  • sriracha
  • fresh minced garlic
  • spices
  • lemon juice

Steps

  1. lay filets out on paper
  2. season with salt and pepper
  3. top sauce on filets
  4. place in oven to bake
